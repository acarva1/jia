What is Jazz in Austin?

A place where Austin jazz musicians can post where and when they'll be performing, and where jazz lovers can always find what shows are happening in the Capitol City

How does it work?

View shows and concerts by day from the Calendar

Learn about Austin's artists at the Musician Profiles page

Discover jazz hot spots on the Venues page

How do I sign up?

Musicians and promoters who have registered an account can log in and create and create events, make a personal profile page, add a bio and manage their upcoming shows
